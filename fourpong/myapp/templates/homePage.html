Welcome to 4Pong, a four player multiplayer pong game where each player takes control of one paddle and tries to bounce a ball past an opponents screen.
Head over to the Game/Lobby tab to begin playing against other players or head to the Leaderboards page to see how you stack up against other players.

How to Play

  1. Use the A and D keys to move your paddle left and right if you are red player.
  2. Use the W and S keys to move your paddle up and down if you are orange player.
  3. Use the J and L keys to move your paddle left and right if you are blue player.
  4. Use the I and K keys to move your paddle up and down if you are green player.
  5. Now block the pong balls from going past your side of the screen and send the ball past a opponents' screen to score points.
  6. First player to 20 points wins, or highest scorer after the timer ends!
